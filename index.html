Course name: Communication Strategies

Our team decided to do the communication strategies topic because we are more knowledgeable about the information and already have a good understanding of the material. They are everyday skills that are crucial to effective and efficient communication in the real world. Through working in smaller groups on certain aspects, we will learn to better our communication and build a relationship as a group.

Our final project deliverable will be a website containing bundles of 5 showing how the communication strategies work together in a real setting. We will also be creating infographics to demonstrate to our audience how they connect.

We wish the future students can make reference to and learn from our project.
